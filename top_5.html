Defining Moment About Ronaldo

APRIL, 2018

Bicycle kick goal at Juventus Stadium

On April 3, 2018, history was made at the Juventus Stadium in Turin, Italy, as Cristiano Ronaldo scored one of the most spectacular goals in the history of football. The goal left spectators in awe and drew praise from players and fans alike. The goal was a bicycle kick, a move that is not easy to execute and requires great skill, athleticism, and timing.

Defining Moment About Messi

APRIL, 2007

His greatest goal?

Few goals in the history of football can compare to this one. Messi slaloms his way past multiple Getafe defenders to score perhaps the greatest goal of his career. It strangely mirrors a goal scored by compatriot Diego Maradona against England in the 1986 World Cup in Mexico.

Defining Moment About Mbappé

December, 2022

Historic World Cup final hat trick

Despite his country’s defeat, Mbappé made history as just the second men’s player to ever score a hat trick in a World Cup final. The treble saw Mbappé clinch the Golden Boot at the tournament with eight goals over seven games.
